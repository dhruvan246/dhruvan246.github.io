Widely regarded as the most intelligent dog breed on earth, the Border Collie is a tireless, brilliant working dog. They are incredible companions — for the right, active owner who can meet their enormous need for activity and mental challenge.

## Overview

| Trait | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Size | Medium (14–20 kg) |
| Lifespan | 12–15 years |
| Coat | Smooth or rough double coat |
| Energy | Very high |
| Good with kids | Yes, with enough exercise |

## Appearance & temperament

Border Collies are athletic, medium-sized herders with an intense, focused gaze ("the eye"). They are hyper-intelligent, sensitive and driven — happiest when they have a job to do.

## Exercise & mental stimulation — the most important part

This is not a dog for a sedentary home:

- Needs **2+ hours** of vigorous exercise daily.
- Just as important: **mental work** — puzzle toys, training, agility, herding games.
- A bored Border Collie becomes anxious and destructive, and may develop obsessive habits.

## Diet & nutrition

- A quality, performance-appropriate diet matched to their high activity.
- Adjust portions to their workload to keep them lean and muscular.

## Training

A joy to train — they learn fast and excel at agility, obedience and trick training. Channel that brainpower constructively; they thrive on learning new tasks.

## Grooming & care

- Brush 2–3 times a week (more during shedding).
- Check ears, trim nails, maintain dental hygiene.

## Health & lifespan

Watch for hip dysplasia, eye conditions (Collie Eye Anomaly), epilepsy and a drug sensitivity (MDR1 gene — ask your vet before certain medications). Responsible breeding and regular vet care matter.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are Border Collies good pets?**
For active, engaged owners — yes, exceptional. For a quiet household — no, they'll be miserable.

**Do they need a job?**
Essentially, yes. Sports, training or work keep them balanced.

## Conclusion

The Border Collie is a phenomenal dog for someone ready to match its energy and intelligence. Give it purpose, exercise and mental challenge, and you'll have the most capable, devoted partner imaginable.
