The Deutsch Stichelhaar, sometimes called the German Roughhaired Pointer, is one of Germany's older and rarer versatile hunting breeds. Developed to point, track and retrieve across varied terrain, it is a hardy working gundog defined by its harsh, bristly coat and steady, hunt-focused temperament. It remains uncommon outside dedicated hunting circles.

## Appearance & temperament

The Deutsch Stichelhaar is a medium-to-large, athletic dog typically standing around 58–70 cm at the shoulder, built for endurance rather than speed. Its defining feature is a coarse, bristly coat ("Stichelhaar" refers to the wiry, needle-like guard hairs) that lies close to the body and provides protection in fields, thickets and water. A beard and eyebrows are characteristic. Coats commonly appear in brown, brown roan or grey with brown markings.

Bred purely as a working gundog, the breed is intelligent, even-tempered and strongly bonded to its handler. It tends to be calm at home but driven and focused in the field, with a pronounced prey and tracking instinct. With proper socialization it is generally good with familiar children, though its working energy means it is best suited to experienced, active owners.

## Diet & nutrition

Feed a complete, balanced diet appropriate to a large, high-activity dog, choosing a formula suited to your dog's life stage. Working individuals during hunting season may need more calories, while less active periods call for fewer to prevent weight gain. Divide daily food into two meals and provide constant access to fresh water. As with other deep-chested breeds, avoid heavy exercise immediately before or after eating. Treats should stay modest, and any diet changes are best discussed with your veterinarian.

## Exercise & training

This is a demanding breed that needs substantial daily exercise: long walks, running, swimming and, ideally, outlets for its natural pointing and retrieving instincts. A bored or under-exercised Stichelhaar can become restless. Mental work matters as much as physical activity, so scent games, retrieving drills and structured tasks are valuable. The breed is intelligent and eager to please, responding well to consistent, reward-based training. Early socialization and reliable recall are important given its strong hunting drive.

## Grooming & care

The harsh coat is relatively low-maintenance. Weekly brushing removes loose hair and debris and helps maintain the coat's protective texture; occasional hand-stripping may be needed to keep it functional. Bathe only as needed to preserve natural oils. Check and clean the ears regularly, as drop ears can trap moisture, and inspect the beard and feet after fieldwork. Routine nail trimming and dental care round out a sensible grooming schedule.

## Health & lifespan

The Deutsch Stichelhaar is generally a robust, working-bred dog with a typical lifespan of around 12 to 14 years. As a large, active breed it can be prone to concerns seen in similar gundogs, including hip dysplasia, ear infections related to its drop ears, and the risk of bloat (gastric torsion) common to deep-chested dogs. Responsible breeders screen breeding stock for hereditary conditions. Routine veterinary checkups, parasite prevention and weight management support a long, healthy life.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is the Deutsch Stichelhaar a good family pet?** It can be a devoted companion for active, experienced owners, but its high energy and strong hunting instincts make it better suited to working or sporting homes than to a sedentary household.

**How much exercise does it need?** A great deal. Plan for vigorous daily activity plus mental stimulation; this breed thrives when given a job and ample opportunity to run, track and retrieve.

## Conclusion

The Deutsch Stichelhaar is a hardy, versatile German gundog prized for its weather-resistant bristly coat and steady working temperament. Rare and purpose-bred, it rewards owners who can meet its considerable exercise and mental needs with loyalty and tireless field ability. For active hunting or sporting households, it makes a capable and dedicated partner.
