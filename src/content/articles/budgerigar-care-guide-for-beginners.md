Small, colourful and full of personality, the budgerigar (or "budgie") is one of the most popular pet birds in the world — and an excellent choice for first-time bird owners. Here's how to give your budgie a happy life.

## Overview

| Trait | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Size | Small (18 cm, ~30–40 g) |
| Lifespan | 5–10 years |
| Social | Very — best kept in pairs |
| Noise | Moderate chirping and chatter |
| Talking | Can mimic words with patience |

## Setting up the cage

- Choose the **largest cage you can** — wider is better than taller.
- Bar spacing should be about 1 cm so they can't get stuck.
- Add several perches of varying widths (natural wood is great for foot health).
- Include toys, a cuttlebone, and food/water dishes.
- Place the cage away from drafts, kitchens (fumes are deadly) and direct sun.

## Diet & nutrition

- A base of high-quality budgie pellets and/or a quality seed mix.
- Daily fresh vegetables: leafy greens, carrot, broccoli, capsicum.
- Occasional fruit as a treat.
- Always provide fresh water and a cuttlebone for calcium.
- **Never feed:** avocado, chocolate, caffeine, or salty foods.

## Taming & socialisation

Budgies are flock animals and crave company:

- Keep at least two if you can't spend lots of time with them.
- Start taming slowly — talk softly, then offer millet from your hand.
- Be patient; trust can take days or weeks.
- Allow daily supervised time outside the cage in a bird-safe room.

## Care & health

- Spot-clean the cage daily and do a full clean weekly.
- Watch for signs of illness: fluffed feathers, lethargy, loss of appetite, laboured breathing.
- Find an avian vet before you need one.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can budgies really talk?**
Some can mimic words and sounds, especially males, with regular gentle repetition.

**Should I get one budgie or two?**
Two is kinder unless you can devote significant daily one-on-one time.

## Conclusion

Budgerigars pack enormous charm into a tiny package. With a spacious cage, a balanced diet and daily interaction, your budgie will become a cheerful, chattering member of the family.
