Intelligent, courageous and devoted, the German Shepherd is one of the world's most capable and popular dogs. From family guardian to police and service work, this versatile breed does it all — for an owner ready to train and exercise them well.

## Overview

| Trait | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Size | Large (22–40 kg) |
| Lifespan | 9–13 years |
| Coat | Dense double coat |
| Energy | High |
| Good with kids | Yes, with training and socialisation |

## Appearance & temperament

German Shepherds are strong, athletic dogs with a noble head and the classic black-and-tan coat (though other colours exist). They are confident, loyal and protective — deeply bonded to their family and naturally watchful of strangers.

## Exercise & training

- Need **1.5–2+ hours** of daily exercise plus mental work.
- Highly trainable and thrive on having a job — obedience, scent work, agility.
- **Early socialisation is essential** to channel their protective instincts well.
- Consistent, reward-based training brings out their best.

## Diet & nutrition

- A quality large-breed diet suited to their age and activity.
- Large-breed puppy food supports healthy joint growth.
- Keep them lean to protect their hips and joints.

## Grooming & care

- Brush 3–4 times a week — they shed heavily, especially seasonally ("German shedders").
- Bathe occasionally, trim nails, and maintain dental care.

## Health & lifespan

The breed is prone to hip and elbow dysplasia, bloat (feed smaller meals, avoid exercise right after eating), and degenerative myelopathy. Choose health-screened breeding lines and keep regular vet appointments.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are German Shepherds good family dogs?**
Yes — loyal and protective, but they need training, socialisation and exercise to thrive.

**Do they shed a lot?**
Very much so. Regular brushing is a must.

## Conclusion

The German Shepherd is an exceptional, devoted companion for an active, committed owner. Provide structure, exercise, training and proper joint care, and you'll have a loyal protector and friend for life.
