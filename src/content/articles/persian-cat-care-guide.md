With their luxurious coats, sweet faces and calm personalities, Persian cats are among the most recognisable felines in the world. But that gorgeous fur comes with real responsibility. Here's everything you need to know.

## Overview

| Trait | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Size | Medium (3–5.5 kg) |
| Lifespan | 12–17 years |
| Coat | Long, thick, requires daily grooming |
| Energy | Low — calm and laid-back |
| Good with kids | Yes, gentle homes preferred |

## Appearance & temperament

Persians have a distinctive flat face (brachycephalic), large round eyes and a long flowing coat in many colours. They are quiet, affectionate lap cats who prefer a calm, predictable environment over chaos.

## Diet & nutrition

- Feed a high-quality cat food rich in animal protein.
- Their flat faces can make some kibble shapes hard to eat — choose appropriate sizes.
- Provide constant fresh water; consider a cat fountain to encourage drinking.
- Avoid overfeeding — Persians aren't very active.

## Grooming — the big one

Persians need **daily brushing** to prevent painful mats and tangles:

- Brush every day with a metal comb.
- Bathe occasionally to keep the coat clean.
- Clean around the eyes daily — Persians are prone to tear staining.
- Schedule professional grooming if matting gets out of hand.

## Care & environment

Persians are indoor cats. They're happy with a cosy, quiet home, a few perches, scratching posts and gentle play. They don't need much exercise but enjoy short play sessions.

## Health & lifespan

Be aware of breed-specific issues:

- **Breathing & eye problems** due to the flat face.
- **Polycystic kidney disease (PKD)** — ask breeders for screening.
- Dental issues and tear duct problems.

Regular vet visits and good grooming go a long way.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are Persian cats high maintenance?**
Yes — daily grooming is non-negotiable. In return you get an affectionate, peaceful companion.

**Do Persians like to be held?**
Many enjoy gentle cuddling but on their own terms.

## Conclusion

A Persian cat is a serene, loving companion for owners willing to commit to daily grooming and attentive health care. Give them that, and they'll be a calm, beautiful presence in your home for many years.
