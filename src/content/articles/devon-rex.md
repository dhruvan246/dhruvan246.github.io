With huge ears, big eyes and a soft curly coat, the Devon Rex looks like a pixie — and acts like a mischievous one too. Playful, clever and intensely people-focused, this breed is a true companion cat.

## Overview

| Trait | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Size | Small–medium (2.5–4.5 kg) |
| Lifespan | 12–16 years |
| Coat | Short, soft, curly |
| Energy | High |
| Good with kids | Excellent |

## Appearance & temperament

Devon Rex cats have a wavy, fine coat, oversized ears and an elfin face. They are mischievous, playful and affectionate — climbing to high perches, learning tricks, and constantly seeking your company and warmth.

## Skin, coat & warmth care

- The fine, sparse curly coat offers little insulation — they get cold and seek out warm laps and sunny spots.
- Their skin can get oily; occasional gentle bathing and ear cleaning help.
- Brush very gently, if at all — the delicate coat can break.

## Diet & nutrition

- A quality diet matched to their active, playful nature.

## Health & lifespan

Watch for **HCM (heart disease)**, patellar luxation, and a hereditary muscle condition (myopathy) in some lines. Choose reputable breeders and keep up regular vet care.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are Devon Rex cats hypoallergenic?**
No — though their unusual coat sheds less, they still produce allergens.

**Are they affectionate?**
Extremely — they're playful "monkey cats" that adore being with their people.

## Conclusion

The Devon Rex is a playful, clever and deeply affectionate companion full of personality. Keep them warm, handle their delicate coat gently, watch their heart health, and enjoy this delightful pixie of a cat.
